The San Gabriel Valley (Spanish: Valle de San Gabriel), often referred to by its initials as SGV, is one of the principal valleys of Southern California, with the city of Los Angeles directly bordering it to the west, and occupying the vast majority of the southeastern part of Los Angeles County. You will find recommendations of where to go when visiting the SGV below. Have fun!

Top Attractions

Arcadia Mall

The Shops at Santa Anita, otherwise known as the Arcadia Mall, is one of the biggest shopping centers in the San Gabriel Valley. With dozens of shops and a large food court, many people spend hours hanging out with their friends here.

Huntington Library

The Huntington Library offers a variety of attractions for guests to visit. From the library containing over 12 million items from the 11th-21st centuries, to the art mueseum holding 45,000 world-renowed pieces of art, paintings, and sculptures, and finally the botanical gardens, guests have plenty of options to choose from in the Huntington Library.

626 Night Market

"626 Night Market is inspired by the famous open-air nighttime bazaars of Asia, where people come together to eat, drink, socialize, and be entertained. Established in 2012, the large-scale market is named after the 626 area code region of San Gabriel Valley, northeast of Los Angeles. The iconic Californian festival features 250+ food, merchandise, crafts, artists, games, live concerts, and entertainment attractions in an epic event that appeals to all ages. 626 Night Market aims to unite and empower the community by serving as a platform for showcasing local talent including performers, chefs, and entrepreneurs." - 626 Night Market

Food Guide

Com Tam Kieu

Asian food is a staple in the San Gabriel Valley and Com Tam Kieu is one of the great Vietnamese resturants that has been here for decades. The prices are affordable for a sit-down resturant and they have many traditional Vietnamese desserts to try after you are done with your meal. However, the Vietnamese Broken Rice is the star of the show at this resturant, but don't ignore the other Vietnamese delicacies that they offer from Pho all the way to Bun Bo Hue!

Address: 633 S San Gabriel Blvd #112, San Gabriel, CA 91776

Pepper Lunch

Hailing from Japan, Pepper Lunch is one of the freshest food options when you are on the search for DIY Japanese Teppanyaki. Pepper Lunch's uniqueness is derived from the plates that you eat on. These plates are hot plates that are used to cook your food right in front of you. For example, their signature item, the beef plate, will have a serving of rice and a ring of raw meat served to you with corn and butter on top. This hot plate cooks that meat right in front of you ensuring the freshest possible meat when eating. This plate plays a big role in their slogan of "Sizzle It Your Way". With only 5 locations in the US, Pepper Lunch is for sure a place you want to visit when you are in the SGV!

Address: 6 West Main St, Alhambra, CA 91801

The Hat

The Hat is home to their world famous pastrami! It is one of the best pastrami you could get your hands on in the San Gabriel Valley and they do not skimp out on your portions. The price to portion ratio at The Hat is unbeatable because of how generous they are. To give insight on how generous they are, a regular order of fries ends up filling up a whole paper bag for some people, so make sure to swing by and experience this for yourself!

Address: 1 Valley Blvd, Alhambra, CA 91801

View Menu Here!

Kyoto Gyukatsu

Kyoto Gyukatsu is home to one of the best gyukatsu's in the SGV! What is gyukatsu you may ask? It is a lightly battered and deep fried beef dish that is left rare on the inside when served. You are then provided a unique grill where you can grill the beef to retain its juciness, while still having that light crisp from the breading. Aside from this, Kyoto Gyukatsu also serves lots of tasty Japanese dishes ranging from Karaage, to Katsu, and even Ramen! If you are ever in the area make sure to stop by and give this place a try!

Address: 150 E Main St STE 100, Alhambra, CA 91801
